How it's started

Foundation

At Indian Mentors, we are proud of our dedicated and passionate team that has been instrumental in our growth and success. Our journey began in April 2019 with just 2-3 tutors and 4-5 students, but their positive feedback and support fueled our continuous expansion.

On November 15, 2019, we officially inaugurated the company, marking our first foundation day. Since then, we have been committed to providing high-quality education and personalized learning experiences to our students.

Our team has grown significantly over the years, and we now have a diverse group of experienced educators and professionals working together to achieve our mission. Each member brings unique skills, knowledge, and perspectives to the table, allowing us to innovate and adapt to the ever-changing educational landscape.

We believe in fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment where every team member feels valued and supported. Our shared passion for education and dedication to student success unite us and drive us forward.

As we continue to grow and expand our reach, we remain committed to maintaining the close-knit, family-like atmosphere that has been the foundation of our success. Join us in our mission to empower students and make a lasting impact on their lives.
